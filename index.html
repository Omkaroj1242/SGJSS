    Our Activities

    This is what we do

    • Women Empowerment

      In agrarian society male-female are like Beljodi of Hal (Bullock pair of a plough). In such situation both should be well versed with all the technological complexities and options for family livelihood.

    • Education

      We believe that the purpose of education is to make the child a self-motivated and independent learner with the ability to think critically.

    • Free Health Checkups, RCH Camps & Vaccination

      Due to lack of hygienic awareness, poor sanitation, bad drinking water quality, gender issues, superstitions, early marriage and pregnancy, frequent pregnancy, lack of balance diet, delay in treatment, infection and lack of cleanliness several health related problems exist in the working area including higher mortality (during delivery and pregnancy).

    • Child Welfare

      SGJSS aims towards lasting improvements in the quality of life of disadvantage and marginalised children living in abject poverty and injustice with interventions focus towards protection and promotion of rights, interests and dignity of children

    • Environment

      The forests of India are ancient in nature and composition. SGJSS is providing wildlife education to the community

    • Agricultural Program

      Farming has always been one of the most important economic activities in India. With about two-thirds of the population engaged in agriculture and agriculture-related activities, it is important to understand the plight of farmers in India and why they are struggling to survive.
